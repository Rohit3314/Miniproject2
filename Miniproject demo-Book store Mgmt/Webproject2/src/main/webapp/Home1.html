Book Store Management

A book shop is a place where different kinds of textbooks or reference books are sold. It is a very important place for all educated persons. Most of the customers of the book shop are book lovers of the all ages. It is mostly familiar to the students. Generally a book shop is seen beside any educational institutions like – school, college or university. Beside them, it is also found in a stationary market place.
